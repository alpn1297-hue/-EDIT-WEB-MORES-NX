SentiMind

AI-powered sentiment analysis platform that captures public opinion, brand perception, and customer emotions across digital channels in real time.

OVERVIEW // 02

Platform Intelligence

SentiMind transforms unstructured data from social media, news outlets, forums, and customer feedback into actionable sentiment intelligence. The platform uses natural language processing and machine learning to detect emotion, tone, and context at scale.

Organizations use SentiMind to monitor brand health, track campaign performance, and identify emerging trends before they become crises. The real-time dashboard provides clear visualizations that help teams make faster, more informed decisions.

Whether tracking public response to policy changes, monitoring competitor sentiment, or measuring customer satisfaction, SentiMind delivers clarity from complexity.

KEY_FEATURES // 03

Core Capabilities

NLP-Powered Analysis

Advanced natural language processing engine that understands context, sarcasm, and multilingual content with high accuracy across Bahasa Indonesia and English.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live dashboards that track sentiment shifts as they happen. Configurable alerts notify teams when sentiment drops below threshold levels.

Multi-Channel Ingestion

Aggregate data from Twitter/X, Instagram, news sites, forums, and internal feedback channels into a single unified view.

Topic Clustering

Automatically group conversations into themes and sub-topics. Identify what people are talking about without manual tagging.

Custom Reporting

Generate branded reports with charts, trends, and key insights. Schedule automated delivery to stakeholders on daily, weekly, or monthly cadences.

API Integration

RESTful API enables seamless integration with existing CRM, BI tools, and internal dashboards. Webhook support for real-time event triggers.

From executive summaries to granular operational metrics, the dashboard scales to support decision-making at every level of the organization.

Dashboard Legislasi

Intelligent monitoring platform that tracks regulatory changes, legislative workflows, and compliance requirements across legal frameworks.

OVERVIEW // 02

Legislative Intelligence

Dashboard Legislasi provides institutions with a comprehensive view of the legislative landscape. The platform aggregates data from parliamentary proceedings, regulatory bodies, and legal databases to create a unified monitoring environment.

Users can track the progress of specific bills, monitor committee activities, and receive alerts when relevant regulations are proposed or amended. The timeline view shows the full lifecycle of legislation from proposal to enactment.

For organizations operating in regulated industries, the platform provides early warning on compliance-impacting changes, helping legal and policy teams stay ahead of evolving requirements.

KEY_FEATURES // 03

Core Capabilities

Bill Tracking

Monitor the status and progress of legislation from introduction through committee review, floor votes, and final enactment. Full timeline visibility.

Regulatory Alerts

Configurable notifications when new regulations are proposed, amended, or enacted. Filter by sector, jurisdiction, or keyword relevance.

Semantic Search

Natural language search across legislative documents, regulations, and policy papers. Find relevant provisions without knowing exact legal terminology.

Legislative Timeline

Visual timeline showing the progression of bills through legislative stages. Track readings, amendments, committee assignments, and vote outcomes.

Version Comparison

Side-by-side comparison of legislative drafts showing additions, deletions, and amendments. Track how provisions change through the legislative process.

Compliance Mapping

Map regulatory requirements to organizational processes and controls. Identify gaps and track remediation efforts across compliance domains.

USE_CASES // 04

Who Uses This Dashboard

GOVERNMENT

Parliamentary Affairs

Government relations teams monitor legislative activity, track bill progress, and brief stakeholders on policy developments.

POLICY

Policy Research

Research institutions and think tanks analyze legislative trends, compare jurisdictions, and produce evidence-based policy recommendations.

FINANCIAL

Banking & Financial Services

Financial institutions monitor changes to banking regulations, tax law, and financial reporting requirements across jurisdictions.
